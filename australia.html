australia

a - sydney

Sydney is a stunning city. Great weather, beautiful beaches and certainly no shortages of things to do here. Sydney opera house, bondi beach and Newtown are all must dos.

b - byron bay

A lovely, chilled-out seaside surf town. Great place to spend time on the beach, great brunches and certainly not lacking in nightlife.

c - fraser island

Fraser Island is made up completely of sand and spans 80 miles. Hire 4x4s and drive along the beach. Do NOT miss Lake Mackenzie, an absolute paradise.

d - whitsundays

A collection of over 70 heavenly islands and a gateway to the great barrier reef. The 2 day boat trips are recommended, including Whitehaven beach and snorkeling.

e - cairns

Cairns is the best place to do a days trip diving in the Great Barrier Reef. It is also the livliest city along the east coast as it is the last stop for most travellers.

f - uluru

Into the outback. Ayers rock is an impressive sight and an absolute must see when in Australia. Uluru offers a completely experience to the coastal towns and cities and its a refreshing change.

hot tips

1 - Plan extra time in Byron Bay! It is very common that people allow 2-3 days here and extend for nearer a week. Take a surf lesson while you're here it's the best place to learn.

2 - Stay at Gilligans in Cairns! Huge hostel, nice rooms, big pool and a bar/nightclub.

3 - Skydive!! Mission Beach and Byron Bay are the popular places to do a skydive, both offering fantastic views. Even if you're sceptical, its definitley worth it!
