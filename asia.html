asia

a - bangkok

Bangkok is a great city to start your south east asia journey. It's one of the regions biggest airports so flying in and out of here is easy. Head down to koh san road in the evening to sample some of Bangkoks nightlife.

b - chang mai

Chang Mai is the most popular location for elephant sanctuarys. Spend a day with some incredible animals and then check out the town square in the evening for dinner and drinks.

c - hanoi

Hanoi is the hub for all of north Vietnam. Use this city as your base to do Ha Long Bay, Cat Ba and Sapa.

d - hoi an

Hoi An is a beautiful riverside town halfway down Vietnam. In addition to the Hai Van pass Hoi An also offers some of the cheapest tailoring you will find.

e - ho chi minh

Ho Chi Minh city is place for some history. War museums that are fascinating and really gives some perspective on the details of the Vietnam War.

f - phuket + koh phi phi

Phuket and Koh Phi Phi is the perfect place for you to spend some time on the beach and blow off some steam. Endless choice of boat parties, pool parties and bar crawls.

hot tips

1 - Oasis cruise tour of Ha Long Bay! A large cruise ship with a bar, huge decking space and 3 hot tubs on board! An incredible 2 day trip.

2 - From Hoi An rent mopeds and ride the Hai Van pass. Stunning views along the mountains that runs for miles, make a day of it and go as far as you wish.

3 - In Koh Phi Phi take a full day boat tour to see the setting of "The Beach" stunning surrounding islands and great snorkeling. An open bar to top off a great day.
