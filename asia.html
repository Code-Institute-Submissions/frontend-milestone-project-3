hot tips

1 - Oasis cruise of ha long bay! a large cruise ship with a bar, huge decking space and 3 hot tubs on board! an incredible 2 day trip

2 - from hoi an rent mopeds and ride the hai van pass. stunning views along the mountains that runs for miles, make a day of it go as far as you wish

3 - in koh phi phi take a boat tour for a day to see the setting of "the beach" stunning surrounding islands and great snorkeling. an open bar to top the day off is a great way to end out the day
