south america

a - rio de janeiro

This city is perfectly picturesque with so many things to do and see. Sugar Loaf mountain, Christ the Redeemer, Copacaban, Ipanema Rocks are just a few of the activities you have to do whilst in Rio.

b - iguazu falls

Iguazu Falls borders Brazil Argentina and Paraguay. You can stand at all 3 borders. The waterfall itself is very impressive and very aesthetic.

c - buenos aires

Buenos Aires may not be the prettiest city, but they sure know how to party. Brilliant nightlife every day of the week. During the daytime, check out some tours your hostel offers and you cant go wrong!

d - mendoza

If you like Red Wine this is the place for you! Mendoza is home to many famous Reds that you may have recognized the name from. Take some winery tours and soak up the beautiful Argentine countryside.

e - bolivian salt flats

After Mendoza you'll likely be heading north towards Macha Picchu. And the salt flats offer a fantastic stop over on that journey. It really is an incredible sight that is difficult to put into words.

f - cusco

Cusco is Peruvian city where you'll begin and end your Macha Picchu tour. The 3 day tour is recommended as it offers excellent views of the mountains and isn't too strenuous. And then you get to Macha Picchu itself, which is simply breathtaking. You CANNOT miss this out.

hot tips

1 - Rio. When doing Sugar Load or Christ The Redeemer, take a hike up them instead. Its challenging in parts but very rewarding and worth it for the views! If you do, remember to buy your gondola ticket for Sugar Loaf before hand otherwise you wont make it to the second mountain. Furthermore, check out the sunset from Ipanema Rocks.

2 - Buenos Aires. Stay at Milhouse hostel. The BEST hostel you will ever stay in. There are 2 locations for this chain both very close to one another and it doesn't matter which one you stay at. There are social events, parties, day trips, city tours all here every single day. You wont find a better place.

3 - Mendoza. Rent bicylces and cycle around visiting wineries. Makes for fun day but just remember to be careful and stay safe!
